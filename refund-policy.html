Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Digital Products Policy

ifeelworld sells digital photography products, including digital prints and downloadable images. Due to the nature of digital products, our refund policy is designed to protect both customers and our intellectual property rights.

2. General Refund Policy

2.1 Non-Refundable Items

Generally, digital products are non-refundable once the download link has been provided or the product has been delivered, as digital products cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense.

2.2 Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If you are unable to download or access the digital product due to technical problems on our end, we will work to resolve the issue or provide a refund.
  • Duplicate Purchases: If you accidentally purchase the same product twice, contact us within 48 hours for a refund of the duplicate purchase.
  • Product Not as Described: If the digital product significantly differs from its description, contact us within 7 days of purchase.
  • Unauthorized Purchase: If your payment method was used without your authorization, contact us immediately.

3. Cancellation Policy

3.1 Before Download

You may cancel your order and receive a full refund if you contact us before downloading or accessing the digital product. Once a download has been initiated, cancellation may not be available.

4. Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this policy excludes your rights under the Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage.

You are also entitled to have the goods repaired or replaced if the goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.

5. Processing Refunds

If your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed
  • Please note that your bank or payment provider may take additional time to credit the refund to your account

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through our Contact Page with the following information:

  • Your order number or transaction ID
  • Reason for the refund request
  • Date of purchase
  • Any relevant screenshots or documentation

We will review your request and respond within 5 business days.

7. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or credit card provider without first contacting us, we reserve the right to dispute the chargeback and may take legal action to recover the funds.

We encourage you to contact us first so we can resolve any issues directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the Website.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or wish to request a refund, please contact us through our Contact Page.
