Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

ifeelworld.com ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is shared, and how we keep it secure.

2. No Cookies or Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics tools, tracking pixels, or any form of behavioural tracking.

We do not track browsing behaviour or collect browsing data.

3. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to process your order and deliver your digital downloads:

Information you provide

  • Name
  • Email address

Information from Stripe (Payment Processor)

Stripe collects certain information required to process payments, including:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Payment method details (we never see full card details)
  • Device information, IP address
  • Billing details (if provided)

We do not store or access your full payment details.

Technical Information from Hosting (Vercel)

Vercel may automatically collect limited technical data such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Server logs

This is used for security, diagnostics, and performance optimization.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Process and complete your order
  • Deliver download links to your purchased digital products
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain website security and performance

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

5. How & Why Your Information Is Shared

We only share information with third-party services that are essential for operating this website:

Stripe (Payments)

Your order information is securely transmitted to Stripe for payment processing. Stripe may collect and store data needed to verify and complete the transaction.

Stripe's Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

Vercel (Hosting Provider)

Technical information (like IP address and logs) may be shared automatically with Vercel so the website can function properly, remain secure, and load efficiently.

Vercel's Privacy Policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

We do not share your information with any other third parties.

6. Security Measures

We take reasonable steps to protect your information.

  • This website uses HTTPS encryption to secure data during transmission.
  • All payment information is handled by Stripe using industry-standard encryption and security practices.
  • We do not store sensitive payment data on our servers.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Ask what information we have
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Ask questions about this Privacy Policy

8. Contact Information

For privacy questions or requests, email us at: hello@ifeelworld.com
